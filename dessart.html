1. Pastries

    Description:

    Pastries are sweet and savory baked goods made with a flaky pastry dough filled with various fillings such as fruit, cream, or meat.
  2. Ice Creams

    Description:

    Ice creams are frozen desserts made with cream, sugar, and flavorings such as fruits, chocolate, or nuts, churned to incorporate air and create a smooth texture.
  3. Sweets

    Description:

    Sweets are confections typically made with sugar, flavorings, and sometimes nuts or fruit, enjoyed as a dessert or snack.
  4. Drinks

    Description:

    Drinks are beverages served either hot or cold, made from various ingredients such as fruits, vegetables, herbs, and spices.
  5. Mojitos

    Description:

    Mojitos are a refreshing cocktail made with white rum, lime juice, mint leaves, sugar, and soda water, served over ice.
  6. Thick Shakes

    Description:

    Thick Shakes are creamy and indulgent beverages made by blending milk, ice cream, and flavorings such as chocolate, vanilla, or fruit.
